About DASBEM Project

The DASBEM (Dynamic Analysis of Structures by Boundary Element Method) project is an advanced software tool developed for over a decade, providing precise time-domain simulations of elastic and viscoelastic half-space structures under seismic SH-waves. Its sophisticated numerical algorithms allow engineers and researchers to model complex interactions with minimal meshing requirements, accurately predict surface responses, and optimize structural designs. DASBEM is widely recognized for its scientific rigor and high computational efficiency.
