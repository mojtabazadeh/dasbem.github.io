About DASBEM

DASBEM (Dynamic Analysis System using Boundary Element Method) is a pioneering research platform for seismic wave propagation and half-space time-domain boundary analysis. Our mission is to blend computational mechanics with modern visualization to simulate seismic SH-wave scattering with unprecedented accuracy.

This project integrates advanced algorithms, dynamic boundary formulations, and real-time analytics to redefine the standards of wave interaction studies in engineering seismology.
