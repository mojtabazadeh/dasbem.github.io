About DASBEM

DASBEM (Dynamic Analysis by the Scaled Boundary Element Method) is an advanced computational framework dedicated to modeling wave propagation and dynamic responses in semi-infinite and infinite media.

The project integrates analytical precision with numerical stability to investigate seismic wave scattering, ground motion analysis, and structural-soil interaction under SH-wave incidence in the time domain.
