About DASBEM

DASBEM (Dynamic Analysis using the Semi-infinite Boundary Element Method) is a research-based computational framework developed to simulate wave propagation and dynamic response of geological and structural systems in the time domain.

This system combines precision in mathematical modeling with efficiency in boundary element computation, focusing on half-space elastodynamics, SH-wave scattering, and wave–structure interaction.

Designed and developed by Saeed Mojtabazadeh and collaborators to push the limits of boundary-based seismic simulation methods.
