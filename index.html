About DASBEM

DASBEM (Dynamic Analysis using the Semi-infinite Boundary Element Method) is an advanced research framework for simulating the propagation and scattering of seismic SH waves in elastic half-space media.

Developed by Saeed Mojtabazadeh, the system focuses on accurate modeling of wave–structure interactions, boundary element formulation, and real-time computational visualization.

DASBEM combines scientific rigor and artistic precision — an elegant fusion of mathematics, mechanics, and imagination.
